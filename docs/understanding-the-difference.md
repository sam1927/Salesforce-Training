      <div class="retro-content-kicker">Level 6</div>
      <h1>Understanding the Difference</h1>
      <p class="retro-content-subtitle">Understanding the difference between mentee profiles, calendar events, and service appointments (sessions) in Salesforce.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="retro-stepper" data-stepper>

      <div class="retro-step-indicator">
        Step <span data-step-current>1</span> of <span data-step-total>6</span>
      </div>

      <div class="retro-step-stage">

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel is-active" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Overview</h2>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>A Mentee's Profile</strong></li>
            <li><strong>Calendar Events</strong></li>
            <li><strong>Service Appointments</strong></li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>1. A Mentee's Profile</h2>
          <p>
            The Mentee Profile is the young person’s <strong>main record</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            You can open this by clicking on the young person’s name.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="/assets/images/understanding-the-difference/open-mentee-profile-view.gif"
              alt="Opening a mentee profile by clicking on the young person's name"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>

          <p>This is where you can find:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>personal details</li>
            <li>funding information</li>
            <li>games and equipment</li>
            <li>the original sign-up form</li>
          </ul>

          <p><strong>This is for information only.</strong></p>

          <p>It does not:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>schedule sessions</li>
            <li>confirm attendance</li>
            <li>record what happened in a session</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>2. Calendar Events</h2>
          <p>
            A Calendar Event is the session in your calendar.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="/assets/images/understanding-the-difference/open-calendar-event-view.gif"
              alt="A calendar event in the mentor calendar"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>

          <p>This shows:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>the date and time</li>
            <li>who the session is with</li>
            <li>the type of session</li>
          </ul>

          <p>
            It is used to <strong>organise and manage your schedule</strong>.
          </p>

          <p>
            It helps you plan the session, but it does <strong>not confirm that the session happened</strong>.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>3. Service Appointments</h2>
          <p>
            A Service Appointment is created automatically when a Calendar Event is made. The Service Appointment is linked to that specific Calendar Event.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="/assets/images/understanding-the-difference/open-service-appointment-view.gif"
              alt="Opening a service appointment from the SA link"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>

          <p>
            You can open it by clicking a link that starts with <strong>SA</strong>.
          </p>

          <p>This is where you:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>confirm attendance</li>
            <li>complete the wrap up</li>
            <li>add session notes</li>
            <li>update the session status</li>
          </ul>

          <p>This is what records what actually happened.</p>

          <p>The system uses this for:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>attendance tracking</li>
            <li>attendance emails</li>
            <li>invoicing</li>
            <li>payment processing</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Simple Way to Remember It</h2>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>A Mentee's Profile</strong> = information about the young person</li>
            <li><strong>Calendar Events</strong> = the "when" and "who with" of a session</li>
            <li><strong>Service Appointments</strong> = what happened in the session</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Key Point</h2>
          <p>
            If you need to read about the young person, go to the <strong>Mentee Profile</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            If you need to check or move a session, use the <strong>Calendar Event</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            If you need to confirm the session took place and complete the wrap up, use the <strong>Service Appointment</strong>.
          </p>
        </section>
