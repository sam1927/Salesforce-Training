        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel is-active" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Overview</h2>
          <p>
            If you need to contact <strong>Senior Leadership</strong> or a specific <strong>department</strong>, you can do this using <strong>Support Tickets</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>To access this section:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>Click <strong>Support</strong></li>
            <li>Click <strong>Support Tickets</strong></li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2 class="retro-important-heading">Important</h2>
          <p>
            <strong>
              Please do NOT log safeguarding concerns through the ticket system.
              The ticket system is for general questions and support requests only.
              If your concern is safeguarding-related, you must use the safeguarding form instead:
            </strong>
          </p>
          <p class="retro-important-link">
            <a href="https://forms.gle/z5gaV3zXVATUjDHJ7" target="_blank" rel="noopener">
              Safeguarding Form
            </a>
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>How to Raise a Ticket</h2>
          <ul>
            <li>Go to <strong>Support</strong></li>
            <li>Click <strong>Support Tickets</strong></li>
            <li>Click <a href="https://mindjam.my.site.com/Portal/s/support-home" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Raise a Case</strong></a></li>
          </ul>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img

              alt="How to raise a support ticket"

              data-lightbox

          </div>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Filling in the Form</h2>

          <p><strong>Case Category</strong></p>
          <p>
            Choose the option that best matches your question (e.g. Finance, Provision, SEND, General Advice).
          </p>

          <p><strong>Priority</strong></p>
          <p>
            <strong>High:</strong> Urgent issues that need attention within the next 24 hours.<br>
            <strong>Medium:</strong> Important but not urgent.<br>
            <strong>Low:</strong> General questions or non-urgent requests.
          </p>

          <p><strong>Subject</strong></p>
          <p>
            Add a short, clear title (e.g. invoice query, mentee match question).
          </p>

          <p><strong>Description</strong></p>
          <p>
            Add a brief explanation of the issue or question.
          </p>

          <p>
            Click <strong>Next</strong> to submit your ticket.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Checking Your Ticket</h2>
          <p>
            To view your tickets, click <a href="https://mindjam.my.site.com/Portal/s/support-home" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>My Cases</strong></a>.
          </p>
          <p>This will show:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>new tickets</li>
            <li>older tickets</li>
            <li>current status</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Replies and Updates</h2>
          <p>
            When someone replies, you will receive an <strong>email notification</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            You can also check the <strong>Chatter</strong> section on the right-hand side of the ticket.
          </p>
          <p>
            This is where updates and replies appear.
          </p>
          <p>
            You can reply there yourself. To see the full history, click <strong>More Comments</strong>.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Key Point</h2>
          <p>
            Use <strong>Support Tickets</strong> for general questions or to contact a department or senior leadership.
          </p>
          <p>
            Do <strong>not</strong> use tickets for safeguarding concerns. Use the safeguarding form instead.
          </p>
        </section>
