    <div class="retro-stepper" data-stepper>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel is-active" data-step-panel>
          <h2>How to Open Update Preferences</h2>
          <p>
            There are <strong>two ways</strong> to access this section.
          </p>

          <h3>Option 1</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>Go to <strong>My Account</strong></li>
            <li>Click <strong>My Account</strong> again</li>
            <li>In the top right, click <a href="https://mindjam.my.site.com/Portal/s/update-preferences" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Update Games and Equipment</strong></a> Flow</li>
          </ul>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/update-games-equipment-preferences/open-update-preferences-form.gif"
              alt="Option 1 for opening Update Preferences"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>

          <h3>Option 2</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>Go to the <strong>Home Page</strong></li>
            <li>Scroll to the bottom</li>
            <li>Click <strong>Update Preferences</strong></li>
          </ul>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/update-games-equipment-preferences/update-game-selection.gif"
              alt="Option 2 for opening Update Preferences"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>

          <p>
            This will open the <strong>Update Games and Equipment</strong> flow.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>What a Flow Means</h2>
          <p>
            A flow in Salesforce is a <strong>step-by-step form</strong> that guides you through a process.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/update-games-equipment-preferences/flow.png"
              alt="Salesforce flow example"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>

          <p>
            In this case, it simply asks you to enter information about your:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>games</li>
            <li>equipment</li>
            <li>interests</li>
            <li>skills</li>
            <li>preferences</li>
          </ul>
          <p>
            Click <strong>Next</strong> to begin.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Step 1: Add Your Games</h2>
          <p>
            You will first see a list of games.
          </p>
          <p>
            Tick the games you currently have access to.
          </p>
          <p>
            Some of the game options may be a little <strong>out of date</strong>. These are being reviewed and updated over time.
          </p>
          <p>
            If you have access to any of MindJam’s most commonly used games, please make sure you tick
            them where relevant, including:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>Roblox</li>
            <li>Fortnite</li>
            <li>Minecraft Java</li>
            <li>Minecraft Bedrock</li>
          </ul>
          <p>
            If a game you use is not listed, use the <strong>Other Games</strong> free text box to add it.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Step 2: Add Your Equipment</h2>
          <p>
            Next, tick the equipment that is relevant to you.
          </p>
          <p>
            If something you use is not listed, use the <strong>Other Equipment</strong> free text box to add it.
          </p>
          <p>
            Equipment options will be updated over time.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Step 3: Add Other Information</h2>
          <p>
            You will then be asked for some additional information. This is to help assigning find better and more suitable mentee matches for you.
          </p>

          <h3>Gender Identity</h3>
          <p>
            You can complete this if you want to. There is also a <strong>Prefer not to say</strong> option.
          </p>

          <h3>Interests</h3>
          <p>
            Add any interests you would like to include.
          </p>

          <h3>Other Games</h3>
          <p>
            There is another box where you can add extra games if you forgot any earlier.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Step 4: Add Your Skills</h2>
          <p>
            You will also see a section called <strong>Mentor Skills</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            This uses a <strong>dual list box</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            To complete this:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>Click the skills that apply to you</li>
            <li>Click the arrow to move them into the chosen section</li>
          </ul>
          <p>
            Only move across the skills that are relevant to you.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Finish the Form</h2>
          <p>
            When you have completed everything, you will see a message confirming that your preferences
            have been updated.
          </p>
          <p>
            Click <strong>Finish</strong>.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/update-games-equipment-preferences/update-equipment-selection.gif"
              alt="Click Finish after updating your preferences"
              class="retro-inline-image"
            >
          </div>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Keeping It Up to Date</h2>
          <p>
            You can update your preferences at any time.
          </p>
          <p>
            <strong>We recommend updating</strong> them if:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>you start using a new console</li>
            <li>you get access to a new game</li>
            <li>your equipment changes</li>
            <li>your skills change</li>
          </ul>
          <p>
            If there is a game or piece of equipment that should be added to the list, please let
            senior leadership know so it can be reviewed.
          </p>
        </section>
