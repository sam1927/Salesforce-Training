      <h1>Wrap Up a Session</h1>
      <p class="retro-content-subtitle">How to complete session notes, close out a mentoring session, cancel a session, or handle a late notice cancellation. Also covers attendance, invoicing, and payment.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="retro-stepper" data-stepper>

      <div class="retro-step-stage">

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel is-active" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Overview</h2>
          <p>
            Wrapping up a session confirms what happened and updates the session record for attendance, invoicing, and payment.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>How to Wrap Up a Session</h2>
          <p>
            Open the <strong>Service Appointment</strong>. This can usually be found on clicking on a hyperlink that contains <strong>SA</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            Click <strong>Wrap Up Session</strong>. This can be found in the top right corner within the Service Appointment.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/wrap-up-a-session/open-session-notes.gif"
              alt="Opening a Service Appointment and clicking Wrap Up Session"
              class="retro-inline-image retro-lightbox-target"
              data-lightbox
            >
          </div>

          <p>
            You can open a Service Appointment from different places in the system, such as the homepage, calendar, or dashboard. Look for a link that starts with <strong>SA</strong>.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/wrap-up-a-session/open-wrap-up-from-session.gif"
              alt="Different places in the system where a Service Appointment can be opened"
              class="retro-inline-image retro-lightbox-target"
              data-lightbox
            >
          </div>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Funding Type</h2>
          <p>
            Next, choose whether the session is:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>Funded</strong></li>
            <li><strong>Self-funded</strong></li>
          </ul>

          <p>
            If you are not sure, you can check with <strong>Finance</strong> or <strong>Senior Leadership</strong>, or log a ticket with <strong>Finance</strong>.
          </p>

          <p>
            In many cases, there will only be one option.
          </p>
          <p>
            If there is more than one, choose the one that matches the arrangement for that mentee.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/wrap-up-a-session/log-session-details.gif"
              alt="Choosing the correct funding type during wrap up"
              class="retro-inline-image retro-lightbox-target"
              data-lightbox
            >
          </div>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Why This Matters</h2>
          <p>
            Wrapping up a session is important because it helps the <strong>system</strong>:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>confirm</strong> what happened</li>
            <li>send automatic <strong>attendance emails</strong></li>
            <li>support <strong>invoicing</strong></li>
            <li>support <strong>payment processing</strong></li>
          </ul>

          <p>
            If a <strong>mentor</strong> does not wrap up a session, they will <strong>not be paid</strong> for that session.
          </p>
          <p>
            <strong>Invoices</strong> will automatically be generated at the <strong>end of each month</strong>. Please make sure sessions are wrapped up before the <strong>final day of each month</strong>.
          </p>
          <p>
            If you are unable to wrap up any sessions for any reason, please let a member of <strong>Senior Leadership</strong> know the <strong>status</strong> of each session you were unable to wrap up (<strong>Attended</strong>, <strong>Cancelled</strong>, and so on), and they will wrap your sessions up for you.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>If You Need to Cancel a Session</h2>
          <p>
            If a session needs to be cancelled in advance, you do not need to wrap it up.
          </p>

          <p>Instead:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>Open the <strong>Service Appointment</strong></li>
            <li>Click <strong>Cancel Session</strong></li>
            <li>Add the reason</li>
            <li>Follow the steps on screen</li>
          </ul>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img
              src="../assets/images/wrap-up-a-session/open-session-recording.gif"
              alt="Cancelling a session from the Service Appointment"
              class="retro-inline-image retro-lightbox-target"
              data-lightbox
            >
          </div>

          <p>
            You will be asked for:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>an internal cancellation note. This will not be shared in any attendance emails.</li>
            <li>a cancellation reason from a dropdown. This will be shared in any attendance emails.</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>If Something Is Missing</h2>
          <p>
            Occasionally, the system may stop you from finishing the wrap up because some information is missing.
          </p>

          <p>In that case:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>Click <strong>Next</strong></li>
            <li>Skip past the wrap up page if needed</li>
            <li>Use the <strong>Admin Details</strong> box to explain the issue briefly</li>
          </ul>

          </p>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>After You Submit</h2>
          <p>
            Once everything has been completed, the <strong>Service Appointment</strong> will update and the <strong>session record</strong> will be complete.
          </p>
          <p>
            This is what the system uses for <strong>attendance</strong>, <strong>invoicing</strong>, and <strong>payment</strong>.
          </p>
