          <p>
            The mentee profile is where you decide whether to accept or reject a mentee.
          </p>

          <div class="retro-image-panel">
            <img

          <p>
            The <strong>Accept</strong> and <strong>Reject</strong> buttons are located at the top right of the mentee's profile.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>What Accepting or Rejecting a Mentee Means</h2>
          <p>
            When a new mentee is assigned to you, the first step is to read their form submission carefully and attend the initial parent/carer meeting.
          </p>
          <p>
            You should only decide whether to accept or reject the mentee after both of these steps have taken place.
          </p>
          <p>
            The form submission on its own is not enough to decide whether the match is suitable.
          </p>

          <h3>Making Your Decision</h3>
          <p>
            If the mentee feels like a good fit, you should <strong>Accept</strong> them. Once accepted, they will be added to your caseload.
          </p>
          <p>
            If the mentee does not feel like a good fit, you should <strong>Reject</strong> them. Once rejected, they will be returned to the assigning team so another match can be considered.
          </p>
        </section>

        <section class="retro-panel retro-step-panel" data-step-panel>
          <h2>Off-Boarding a Mentee</h2>
          <p>
            If a mentee is leaving the service, you can off-board them from their profile.
          </p>
          <p>
            When you off-board a mentee, you will need to:
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>Select a <strong>reason</strong> for why they are leaving the service</li>
            <li>Enter an <strong>end date</strong> for when they should be removed</li>
          </ul>
          <p>
            Once submitted, the mentee will be removed from your mentee list from that date.
          </p>
